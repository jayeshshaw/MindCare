Chipur is a blog dedicated to helping people with anxiety, depression, and bipolar through learning. The blog was founded by Bill White, a mental health professional, and features hundreds of articles on mood disorders and the science behind them.

Purple Persuasion is a mental health blog by Charlotte Walker, a service user with bipolar disorder. Charlotte created the blog to connect with others in similar situations, and it documents her journey and thoughts on living with bipolar.

Purple Persuasion
